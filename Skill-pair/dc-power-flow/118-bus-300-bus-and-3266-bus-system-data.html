Abstract

The dataset consists of data for the IEEE 118-bus system, IEEE 300-bus system, and a modified 3266-bus system based on the practical provincial grid of China for unit commitment. The dataset includes one year of load information, renewable energy generation data, and the necessary parameters for solving the unit commitment problem, such as units, lines, and buses.

Instructions:

The dataset is for the paper "A Multi-timescale Learn-to-Optimize Method for Unit Commitment with Renewable Power." The data is stored in three folders: **118-bus**, **300-bus**, and **3266-bus**. In the **118-bus** and **300-bus** folders, `renew_power_data.csv` represents the predicted renewable power generation for one year, and `renew_gen_para` includes the location and installed capacity of renewable power generation units. For additional parameters of the IEEE 118-bus and IEEE 300-bus systems, please refer to Alinson S. Xavier, Aleksandr M. Kazachkov, Ogün Yurdakul, Jun He, Feng Qiu. "UnitCommitment.jl: A Julia/JuMP Optimization Package for Security-Constrained Unit Commitment (Version 0.4)". Zenodo (2024). DOI: 10.5281/zenodo.4269874.  

 

In the **3266-bus** folder, `system_para.mat` contains information about the units, buses, and lines in the system, while `load_renew_data.csv` represents the predicted load demand and renewable power generation for one year.
