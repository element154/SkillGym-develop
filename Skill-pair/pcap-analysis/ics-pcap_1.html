What is ICS-pcap

A service for better visibility on networking issues in Kubernetes clusters by detecting traffic denied by iptables.

This is a collection of PCAPs for ICS/SCADA Utilities and Protocols

This document contains a collection of PCAPs (Packet Capture files) and additional notes where PCAPs are still needed, specifically for ICS (Industrial Control Systems) and SCADA (Supervisory Control and Data Acquisition) utilities and protocols. The credit for these PCAPs goes to contributors including digitalbond, 4sics, netresec, scadahacker, Wireshark sample captures, shodan.io, kargs.net, Oak Ridge Datasets, and many others. Users are encouraged to contribute by submitting pull requests with additional PCAP files or information, or by creating an issue for corrections or new uploads.
